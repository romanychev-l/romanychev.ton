While waiting at Savyolovsky station, I decided to calculate how much time I spent on trains in 2025:

83 hours for work on long-distance trains (SPB - MSK)
30 hours on commuter trains (MSK - Dubna)
50 hours on a train to Crimea and back for a conference
34 hours on commuter trains (Baltiyskaya - University)
38 hours during summer travel (Mineralnye Vody - MSK - Ivanovo)
15 hours (SPB - Ivanovo)

Total: 250 hours, or 10 days, or 2.7% of the year 2025.
